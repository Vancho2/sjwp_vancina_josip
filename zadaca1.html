Quick Summary

Jo "Jay" Ja-hyeon wants to ride bikes because he looks up to his uncle Jo Mahyeon who was a professional cyclist.
Jay received a bike from his uncle and has been obsessed with cycling ever since.
Later Jay finds out about an event called League of Streets,
where cyclists form teams to compete against each other.
This event is when Jay starts to make a name for himself.
